How Skipily helps you

Find boatyards & service partners near you

Boatyards, repair shops, sailmakers, fuel docks, painters – thousands of providers across Europe on an interactive map. Sorted by distance, filtered by category and brand, with real reviews.

  • Mediterranean, North Sea and Baltic covered
  • Turn-by-turn directions straight in Apple Maps
  • Phone, email, website with one tap
⭐ Unique in the industry

Original parts at the touch of a button

From any equipment entry straight to the matching spare part – sorted into three clear categories:

  • Original parts – 1:1 match via the part number
  • 1:1 alternatives – same manufacturer & model, should fit directly
  • Other matching equipment – sensible add-ons

No more hours spent searching for the right impeller or water filter.

Service request in 10 seconds

You tap "Request" on an equipment entry – Skipily automatically builds a complete briefing: brand, model, serial number, dimension sheet, installation and maintenance data, photos. You just pick the service partner.

  • Briefing generated automatically in 6 languages
  • Photos and sail dimension sheet attached directly
  • Preview and edit before sending

Stay on top of things – before something breaks

Maintenance intervals per piece of equipment. Skipily shows you with status lights what's due:

  • ● Red – overdue
  • ● Yellow – due within the next 30 days
  • ● Green – all good

Push reminders in good time before the next inspection – never forget anything again.

Ask the AI assistant – it knows your boat

The Skipily assistant knows your equipment and answers specific questions: from "Which oil does my Yanmar 3JH5E need?" to "How do I bleed the diesel engine?".

  • Damage photo analysis (Skipily Plus)
  • The answer can be forwarded straight to a service partner
  • Context from your boat and equipment data

Real reviews from real owners

Every service partner with reviews, photos and contact details. At a glance you see which yard specialises in Yanmar engines and which sailmaker has the best reviews for genoas.

  • Only verified reviews from real customers
  • Trusted-provider badge for vetted businesses
  • Products and special offers visible right away
