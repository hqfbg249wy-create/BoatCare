## Description (max 4000 characters)
Skipily is the all-in-one app for boat owners — whether you sail or motor, weekend cruise or cross oceans.

YOUR BOAT AT A GLANCE
- Manage all your boat data: dimensions, engine, sails, year built
- Document your complete equipment with photos, serial numbers and measurements
- Store sail measurements and technical details in one place

MAINTENANCE MADE EASY
- Automatic reminders for upcoming maintenance intervals
- Complete maintenance history for every piece of equipment
- Find the right service partner near you with a single tap

FIND SERVICE PARTNERS
- Interactive map with boatyards, workshops, sailmakers and dealers
- Search by category and brand — Skipily shows you who knows your engine, electronics or sails
- Ratings, contact details and navigation right from the app
- Save favorites for quick access

AI ASSISTANT
- Ask the Skipily assistant any question about your boat
- From "What oil does my Volvo D2-40 need?" to "How do I fix osmosis blisters?"
- Answers based on current maritime expertise

SPARE PARTS & SHOP
- Find matching spare parts directly from your equipment list
- Smart search with AI-powered alternative suggestions
- Order from specialized marine dealers

MULTILINGUAL
Skipily is available in 6 languages: English, German, French, Dutch, Spanish and Italian.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your data belongs to you. Skipily uses no tracking cookies, no ads, and never sells your data. Servers in the EU. GDPR compliant.
